Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Planning

    Make sure to plan everthing. From where to stay, to where to eat, this will be the most important factor of having a good trip.

  2. Food

    Make sure to eat a lot of food and a lot of good food. Try something new and do not be afraid.

  3. Activities

    Make sure you make the most of the activities you do. There will be a lot to do, so make sure you plan out a timely schedule of when to do what.
