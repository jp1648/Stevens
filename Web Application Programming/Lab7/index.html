My Travel Destinations

Bahamas

The beaches are extremely nice. The sun is always shining. You can visit Atlantis which has super cool water slides like the leap of faith which is a straight shot slide that takes you through an underground aquarium.

  1. Atlantis
  2. Nassau
  3. Bimini

New York City

What doesn't New York have? From a diverse culture of many people, the food scene is like no other in the country. Get the best pizza, ramen, crepes, sushi, you name it, and it will be here in the Big Apple.

  1. Chelsea
  2. Brooklyn
  3. Financial District

Colorado

IF a bustling city is not your scene, Colorado is the place to visit for nature lovers. From snowy mountains to perfect summer weather, Colorado has alot to offer for all types of adventurors.

  1. Denver
  2. Colorado Springs
  3. Boulder
